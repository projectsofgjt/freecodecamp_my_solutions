Here's a time line of Louis Rossmann's life:

  • 1988 - Born
  • 2007 - Opened his repair shop, Rossmann Repair Group, in New York City
  • 2011 - Joined Youtube with his primary channel, at this time it primarily covered doing repairs to Apple computers that were not possible accoring to apple
  • 2015 - Posted his first video directly about right to repair
  • 2017 - Started recording right to repair related hearing all around the contry and posting videos with his commentary and criticisms
  • 2018 - Gained popularity after CBC News did an investigative piece on Apple's repair sytem and compaired it to using Rossmann's shop
  • 2020 - Appeared in right to repair campains about farming machinery
  • 2021 - Started a 501c4 nonprofit called Repair Preservation Group Action Fund and a GoFundMe that raised over $750,000 for right to repair to be passed
  • 2022 - Decided to close the New York City locations, and move operations to Florida
